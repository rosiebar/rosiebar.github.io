Mapping Museums

For this personal project, I explored and visualised a dataset from the Mapping Museums research project (↗)

This dataset contains information on over 4,000 museums across England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland, including details on each museum's name, location, size, subject matter, and the index of multiple deprivation (a metric used in the UK for measuring the affluence of different geographic regions) for the area the museum is located.
